WELCOME TO MY GAME

how to play

  1. Players roll the dice (player one starts)
  2. The dice number will be added to your current score
  3. You can roll the dice again to add points to your current score
    or you can hold your current score
  4. If your dice lands on 1. it's the other player's turn
  5. WIN by reaching 100 scores
